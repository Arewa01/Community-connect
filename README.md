# 🌐 Community Connect – Where Interests Become Communities

**Community Connect** is web app that helps people discover and engage with trusted, interest and location based communities. Whether you're into parenting, tech, fitness, faith, or finance — connect, collaborate, and save smarter with people who share your passions. You can also find people in your neighbourhood, and get real time updates.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 📢 Real-Time Updates
Stay in the loop with instant posts, announcements, and reminders — no more scattered info or missed messages.

### 💰 Group Savings
Join bulk deals, relevant to your interests or location, from baby products to food, books, tools, and more.

### 🧭 Interest and Neighbourhood Based Discovery
Communities are curated by your passions,and your postcode. Discover, join, or create communities around what matters to you.

### 🔒 Private & Safe Spaces
Each community has moderated spaces with optional admin controls, private messaging, and clear boundaries.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js / Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
